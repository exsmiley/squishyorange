How to Play

Two games to play! Use arrow keys to move!

Catch the Squish

One player is the squishy orange! Everyone else will chase the squish until someone catches the squish. Catching the squish yields many points!

Squish Zombies

The squish will infect others with... THE SQUISH. The squish can tag non-squishies in order to make them squishy. Try to be the last one standing!
